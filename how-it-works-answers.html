Inputs The one thing nianav OS asks of you. Everything after this is derived.

A short set of answers, given once.

Project Zero asks a small number of plain questions about your company, your market, your product, and your stage. You answer in your own words. Nothing technical, no framework knowledge, no forms to decode. We tailor the questions to your situation, so the set is short and every answer earns its place.

From here on, nothing is retyped. Every layer that follows, the standards, the requirements, the procedures, the running system, is derived from what you said here.

Whatever you tell Project Zero decides everything downstream. Which laws apply, which procedures switch on, and who is accountable. Follow it through the next four steps and watch each answer cascade.
