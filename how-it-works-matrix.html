Mapping Standards decomposed into individual, trackable, pre-mapped requirements.

87 requirements. 132 clauses. Nothing left dangling.

The standards from step two are not prose to be read. They are decomposed into individual, trackable requirements. nianav OS pre-maps each one so the obligation, the procedure, the evidence type, and the owner are linked from the start.

That is what makes gaps visible in real time. If a clause has no procedure, or a procedure with no evidence, the matrix shows it immediately, not at the audit.

Clause
ProcedureThe documented process that satisfies it.
Evidence typeThe record that will prove it was done.
