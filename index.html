Built for the frameworks you answer to

Nobody hands you the rulebook. It's already built in.

In a regulated industry the rules are real long before anyone tells you them, and most founders meet them the expensive way, mid-audit, when a gap has already cost time and money. nianav OS knows the rulebook up front. The rules live inside the work, invisible while you operate, so the evidence an auditor asks for is produced as you go, not assembled in a scramble the week before.

Specificity is the trust signal.

More than eighty frameworks are already in the library: ISO 27001, GDPR, NIS2, ISO 14971, and the rest. Every one is already wired in; you switch on only what your scope demands, and add the next the day you need it. No rebuild, no migration. The system stays exactly as big as you are.
