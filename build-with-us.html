Where we are The honest version.

The engine works. The product is being built now.

nianav OS is built engine first. The part that runs regulated work, the procedures, records, corrective actions, and the evidence they produce, already runs from end to end. It is held up by a large suite of tests.

The first version a person can log into and use is being built now. That is where our focus sits today.

We build in the open. You can follow the thinking as it happens, here and on LinkedIn, and you can help decide what gets built next.

We would rather show you the real thing, early, than sell you a finished story.

Who we are looking for A small number of companies, chosen with care.

Medical device companies, in the window before scale.

The best time to put this in place is after the product works and before serious scale. Most companies miss that window. Once it closes, it stays closed. If the four below sound like you, we should talk.

You make a medical device.Hardware, software, or both. MDR or IVDR is already your world, or about to be.
You are past the first proof.The product works. You are heading into your first serious scale-up, the moment everything starts to get heavier at once.
You can feel the foundation is not right yet.You have some compliance structure, and you know it will not hold. You would rather build it right now than rebuild it later.
You want to shape the tool.You would rather influence what gets built than wait for a finished product that was designed around someone else.
If that is your company, this was built for you. We are working with founders and operations leads who know the foundation matters, and want to build it right, with us, now.
