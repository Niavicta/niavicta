Derived From a handful of answers to the precise regulatory surface you sit on.

Your standards, activated automatically.

Every framework nianav OS can run is already built into the platform. When you declare your scope, the ones that apply to you are not handed over as a PDF to read and re-key. They activate. Each clause becomes a live requirement, the procedure that satisfies it switches on, and a named owner and reviewer are assigned to it.

You run only what your scope demands. The rest stays dormant until the day you need it, then switches on with no rebuild and no migration. And it is not only quality and manufacturing. Your data duties, your AI obligations, your financial rules and your employment law land in exactly the same place, wired into the work.
