nianav OS

One operating system for regulated startups.

The short version: nianav is nianav OS, Niavicta's operating system for regulated startups. This page explains the problem it solves, how the system is put together, why it exists, and who it is built for.

Start with the problem Under the hood
Simple systems
Serious impact.
The problem Fragmentation is expensive long before the first audit.

Most teams do not lose because they are careless. They lose because the work is scattered.

Procedures live in one place, evidence in another, decisions in email, and status in a project tool. People translate between systems all day, then try to reconstruct what happened when pressure arrives.

nianav OS starts by connecting that reality. It gives regulated startups a shared operating model so the work, the rule, the owner, and the evidence stay linked from the beginning.

Under the hood One data layer. Role-based views.

Everything is one record, rendered for each role.

Underneath the whole system there is one data layer. Every procedure, record, and piece of evidence lives there once, as a single source of truth.

What each person sees is a view onto that one record. The production operator, the QA officer, and the auditor are not looking at separate copies that have to be kept in sync. They are looking at the same record, rendered the way their role needs to read it.

So nothing is duplicated and nothing drifts. Update the record once and every role’s view reflects it. The work and the evidence are the same object, seen through different lenses.

How it works Your answers cascade through five layers. Every operational thing traces back to something you said.
Step 01 · Inputs

Your answers.

A short set of answers about your company, market, product, and stage, gathered once in Project Zero. The only thing you provide.

Open step 1 Step 02 · Derived

Applicable law & standards.

The exact regulations and standards that apply to you are resolved from your answers. No manual mapping, no guesswork.

Open step 2 Step 03 · Mapping

Compliance matrix.

Every clause is pre-mapped to a procedure, an evidence type, and an owner. Gaps surface in real time, before any work starts.

Open step 3 Step 04 · Operations

Procedures.

The procedures you actually need switch on from the Niavicta library, tailored to your answers. The rest stay off.

Open step 4 Step 05 · Live

Your operating system.

Running and audit-ready. Every operational step traces back to an answer you gave on day one.

Why it was built The rules arrive earlier than most teams expect.

Regulated startups need scaffolding before the burden shows up.

Companies often meet the cost of compliance at the exact moment they can least afford to improvise: an audit, a submission, a milestone review, a quality issue, or diligence from an investor or partner.

nianav OS turns that pressure into leverage by making the company easier to run, easier to prove, and easier to grow with confidence.

Who it's for The people who feel the weight of the process most directly.

Built for teams that need structure without losing speed.

If you are a founder, CEO, COO, quality lead, compliance lead, or product and operations leader in a regulated company, nianav OS gives you a clearer operating base.

When the company starts carrying more evidence, more oversight, and more moving parts, the system should help the team stay aligned instead of adding another layer of friction.
