Health AI

Health AI needs operating control before AI starts executing work.

When AI moves from answering questions to helping execute work, the operating system needs to show what happened and who was accountable.

Direct answer: For health AI startups, nianav OS connects AI governance, permitted scope, human accountability, evidence, and review into the operating trail.
What this means A focused resource page for one search intent.
01

Scope

AI-assisted work needs permitted boundaries, not informal usage.

02

Human accountability

A named owner and review path remain attached to the work.

03

Evidence

The record should show what AI touched, what data was used, and what review happened.

Context How this connects to nianav OS.

Health AI companies face a double burden: product evidence and AI governance evidence. Those cannot sit in disconnected fragments.

nianav OS treats AI as part of the operating model, so AI-assisted work stays inside a controlled procedure and record structure.
